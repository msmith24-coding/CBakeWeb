Overview

What is CBake?

CBake is a cross-platform build solution that manages the build process for your C/C++ projects. The only requirement that CBake has is having a GCC compiler installed on the system. CBake has many built in functions to make compiling a breeze. Also, you can easily define and identify variables in the CBakefile source. History of CBake

CBake was originally developed in Febuary of 2023 by Michael Smith during his sophomore year at Pennsylvania Western University at Edinboro. Smith started developing the project because he felt that "Make and CMake's syntax is unnessessarily confusing." (Smith, 2023) Therefore, he developed CBake to solve this problem. Smith also stated that "[CBake] was developed to solve a personal problem, making the Makefiles for my university assignments." (Smith, 2023).
